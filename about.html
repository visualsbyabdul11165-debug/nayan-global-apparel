Who We Are

Built on Craft,
Driven by Quality

Nayan Global & Apparel was founded in Sialkot, Pakistan — a city with a centuries-old legacy of exceptional textile manufacturing. What started as a small workshop serving local markets has grown into a full-scale B2B manufacturing operation trusted by leading retailers across the UK, Europe, the US and beyond.

We specialise in producing premium T-shirts, hoodies, shorts, and activewear for fashion brands, retailers, and wholesalers. Every garment we make is a reflection of our obsession with quality — from the thread count to the final folded product ready for retail.

Our clients range from independent streetwear labels placing their first 50-piece order to established retailers sourcing 50,000+ units per season. We treat every order with the same level of care and precision.
